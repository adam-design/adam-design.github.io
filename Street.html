Smart Meets Wallet

Losing track of your wallet is now a problem of the past. The SmartWallet by StreetSmart Brand turns your wallet into a smart device.

Find your lost SmartWallet quickly using the accompanying iPhone or Android app. If you have lost your phone, the SmartWallet can help you find that, too! SmartWallet also comes with a 1000mAh battery backup, allowing you to charge your phone on the go.

"There are times when we forget our wallet at home ... but worry no more as SmartWallet will solve all your problems."

Hammad Saleem, gadgetreview.com

Design

The SmartWallet is designed to be thin and trendy. We went to great lengths to source leather materials that look sharp and hold up to wear and tear. To protect the technology inside, we wrapped our battery and circuitry in a polymer case that is both waterproof and durable.

"SmartWallet wins points for style."

Kim Lachance Shandrow, Entrepreneur Media, Inc.
